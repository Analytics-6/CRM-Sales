### About The Project

The Situation - Played the role of a Sales Analyst for a tech company that specializes in selling computer hardware to large businesses

Given Task - To perform analysis of last year's sales, calculating key statistics on the health of the sales pipeline, the performance of sales agents, the popularity of the products, and the company's customers

1st Objective - Pipeline metrics

- Assessed the overall sales pipeline by looking at opportunities by month, time to close, win rate, and product data
  
- Calculated the number of sales opportunities created each month
  
- Identified the month with the most opportunities

- Compared closed deals versus won deals


2nd Objective - Sales agent performance

- Assessed the performance of sales agents, their managers, and regional offices

- Calculated the win rate for each sales agent to find the top performer

- Calculated the total revenue by agent to see who generated the most

- Calculated win rates by manager to determine which managerâ€™s team performed best


3rd Objective - Product analysis

- Analyzed the sales performance and quantity sold of the company's product portfolio

- Calculated the average difference between "sales_price" and "close_value" for each product, and noted if the results suggested any data issue

- Calculated total revenue by product series to compare their performance
  

4th Objective - Account analysis

- Analyzed the company's accounts to get a better understanding of the team's customers

- Calculated revenue by office location to identify the lowest performer
